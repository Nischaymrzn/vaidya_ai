# Vaidya_AI 

## Project Introduction
Managing personal health effectively can be challenging, with medical records, prescriptions, and vitals often scattered across different platforms. **Vaidya_AI** is a web-based intelligent health companion designed to centralize personal health information, track vital metrics like blood pressure, BMI, and sugar levels, and provide actionable insights based on user data.  

The platform helps users maintain a complete, structured health history in one accessible location, empowering them to recognize potential health risks early and improve communication with healthcare professionals. With a focus on simplicity and usability, Vaidya_AI supports users of all age groups in managing their health effectively.

---

## Key Features
- **Secure Login & Registration:** Protect personal health information with safe authentication.  
- **Home Dashboard:** Quick overview of key health metrics, recent records, and risk indicators.  
- **Medical History Management:** Organize prescriptions, lab reports, and records in one place.  
- **Health Metrics Tracking:** Monitor BMI, blood pressure, sugar levels, and other vitals.  
- **Symptom Logging:** Record symptoms over time to track patterns and potential risks.  
- **Personalized Insights:** Receive lifestyle suggestions and early health risk identification.  
- **Analytics Dashboard:** Visual trends and graphs for better understanding of long-term health.  
- **Disease Risk Analysis:** AI-powered estimation of possible health risks based on user data.  
- **User Profile Management:** Update personal details, manage records, and control privacy settings.

---

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js (interactive dashboards, forms, and responsive UI)  
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express.js (API handling, authentication, business logic)  
- **Database:** MongoDB (secure storage of records, metrics, and logs)
